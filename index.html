Bloom™ offers an exciting and powerful process of self-discovery that promotes healing and wholeness. Rose Meenach, a Cincinnati-based certified/licensed Transpersonal Hypnotherapist and founder of Bloom, utilizes a specialized form of Transpersonal Hypnotherapy called Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique (QHHT).

Over recent decades, advances in Quantum Physics have yielded a growing body of evidence supporting the idea that the thoughts we have are what drive the reality we experience. Bloom uses QHHT to delve more deeply into our thoughts via our Subconscious mind and our Superconscious, or Higher Self, to identify opportunities for personal development, growth and wellness.

In addition to individual applications, Bloom’s Transpersonal Hypnotherapy modalities when coupled with Mindfulness techniques have proven to be effective practices for the workplace. These methods continue to gain increased attention in corporate environments for improving Team-Building, Leadership Development, and opening minds to fuel Creative Innovation. Bloom offers practices to individuals and to organizations and corporate workplace groups.
